About

Currently serving as a Senior Wireless Research Scientist at Amazon Lab126 in Sunnyvale, CA. Recognized with the Builders Build Award by Amazon Lab126 in 2023 and 2025 for work on miniaturized On-PCB Electromagnetic Bandgap (EBG)-based components and Hardware-Sage: Gen-AI Hardware design co-pilot. Two-time recipient of the Honorable Mention Award at the IEEE Antennas and Propagation Symposium (2017, 2019).

An active contributor to the scientific community, serving as a reviewer for IEEE AWPL, IEEE TAP, PIERs, IET EL, and IET MAP. Held the role of Vice President of Eta Kappa Nu, Epsilon Beta Chapter, the IEEE Honor Society, in 2019.

Instrumental in shipping over 100 million devices, leveraging expertise in designing cost-effective antenna systems, EMI/EMC/RF desense solutions, RF immunity strategies, and SIPI analysis for high-speed interfaces and power nets. Holds 5 US patents with 4 filed at Amazon Lab126, and has published more than 30 research papers cited over 1000 times. Granted U.S. permanent residency in 2022 in recognition of significant contributions to the field.

Antenna Design

RF Desense

Receiver sensitivity degradation analysis, cascaded noise figure, spectrum capture methodology, multi-radio coexistence.

EMI / EMC

Electromagnetic interference mitigation, EMC compliance, shielding effectiveness, noise coupling analysis.

🔬

SIPI Analysis

Signal integrity and power integrity for high-speed interfaces, power delivery network design, PDN impedance optimization.
