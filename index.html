About

Currently serving as a Senior Wireless Research Scientist at Amazon Lab126 in Sunnyvale, CA. Recognized with the Builders Build Award in 2023 and 2025 for work on miniaturized On-PCB Electromagnetic Bandgap (EBG)-based components and Hardware-Sage: Gen-AI Hardware design co-pilot. Two-time recipient of the Honorable Mention Award at the IEEE Antennas and Propagation Symposium (2017, 2019).

Earned B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Nirma University, India (2013), B.A. in Indian Classical Music from Gandharv Vidyalaya (2014), M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State University (2017), and Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from ASU (2020).

Instrumental in shipping over 100 million devices, leveraging expertise in designing cost-effective antenna systems, EMI/EMC/RF desense solutions, RF immunity strategies, and SIPI analysis for high-speed interfaces and power nets. Active reviewer for IEEE AWPL, IEEE TAP, PIERs, IET EL, and IET MAP.

Research Interests

📡 Antenna Design

Miniaturized antennas, MIMO systems, on-PCB EBG structures, multi-band antenna systems for IoT and consumer electronics.

📻 RF Desense

Receiver sensitivity degradation analysis, cascaded noise figure, spectrum capture methodology, multi-radio coexistence.

⚡ EMI / EMC

Electromagnetic interference mitigation, EMC compliance, shielding effectiveness, noise coupling analysis.

🔬 SIPI Analysis

Signal integrity and power integrity for high-speed interfaces, power delivery network design, PDN impedance optimization.
